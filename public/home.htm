Welcome!

Hello, my name is Peter Watters. I took an unusual path to a career in programming- I received a full scholarship to Washington University in St Louis for Painting. After graduating with a BFA, I spent the next six years working as a Printer - I was printing the original DOS manuals for Microsoft in the early 1980's.


I began taking mathematics classes at the University of Washington in preparation for a degree in Engineering. I took a class in Fortran to fulfil a prerequisite and found that I loved programming. I was eager to start so I signed up for the Boeing Computer Services' nine month technical school for business programming and began my career at Boeing. I worked there eight years.


For the last 19 years I have been a software developer at Applied Voice and Speech Technologies. I worked on the CallXpress Product- The code base is close to 30 years old- there are close to 3 million lines of c++ code.
